How to use

The thought of a family member, a friend, or even a valuable item going missing can be terrifying. Our service helps find both missing people and objects. If something or someone you care about is missing, then,

  • Register the missing person with us.
  • We will contact you for a background check.
  • Once verified, the missing person is added to our database of missing people.
  • When volunteers report a suspected missing person or object, our service identifies a potential match within our database.
  • If a match is found we will report to the local authorities and contact you.
Register a Missing Person or Item

If you've found a missing person or object, your help can make a big difference. Our service allows you to report found items or individuals, ensuring they are safely reunited with their rightful owners. Here's how you can assist:

  • Report What You Found.
  • We will cross-verify the report to check its validity.
  • Once verified, our service identify a potential match with our database of missing people and objects.
  • If a match is found we will report to the local authorities and contact the family/owner.
